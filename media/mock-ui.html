Can a temporary resident apply for employment insurance?

Yes, temporary residents can apply for employment insurance (EI) if they meet specific criteria.

According to the Employment Insurance Act, Section 7(1), benefits are payable to insured persons who meet eligibility requirements. A critical requirement is that the applicant must have a valid Social Insurance Number (SIN) and be authorized to work in Canada.
